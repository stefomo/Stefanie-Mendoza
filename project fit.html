FitBuddy



A moblie fitness app to join with friends and get stronger together



  • Scope : UX/UI, Web Design, Design Course

  • Duration : 9 Weeks

  • Team : Lisa Z. & Felicity Y.

check out the moblie website -> here




OverView


Have you ever made a new year resolution to get fit but stopped going to the gym after the second day?

FitBuddy wants to help you plan your next workout with your friends so you can both stay on top of your fitness game! You can schedule a workout on your own OR choose to tag along with your friend’s upcoming workout. By checking in to different workout studios, you can even earn rewards like gym bags and water bottles.

Problem

Staying active and working out regularly is a fundamental necessity to improve one’s health. However, maintaining the motivation to consistently work out can be difficult as life gets hectic and the workouts become unenjoyable, causing most people to quit or stop going to the gym after 24 weeks. Ultimately, many people quit before reaching their fitness goals due to a lack of accountability and support. So our team wanted to create a fitness app that could make working out more social and rewarding.
