Diabetes Education



Team : Allison P. , Stephanie K. , & Vidya R.

Started : June 23, 2020 - Ongoing

Role : Product Designer


Overview : We know that sometimes, seeing large amounts of numbers can be overwhelming, confusing, and scary for people with diabetes. My team and I in the Diabetes Design Initiative are creating an intuitive and easily understandable visualization of data that Pump/CGM users get from their devices. This interactive education tool will help people with diabetes better understand the relationship between glucose and insulin data.
