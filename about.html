About Me

Hello There, I’m Stefanie Mendoza from Orange County, CA, currently a student at UC San Diego studying speculative design and practicing to become a Product + User experience designer!

I have always been curious and eager to explore the world around me and create art to express myself. As a designer I enjoy inventing concepts, taking on new challenges and being able to see my ideas come to life! Within Product design, I have used my skills to solve complex problems to create inclusive and accessible user experiences.
